Crimp Tool

I didn't know how to do this. I solved it the following way.

This problem was straightforward. Our operators were using large, unwieldy crimpers to crimp fairly large power cables for a unit we produced. These were so inconvenient that they usually required two people to use-- it was inefficient. At first, I looked into purchasing an automatic crimper for that size wire. The prices were absurdly expensive for an automatic air pressure crimper. Seeing this, I began designing my own method to crimp the cables. This is image 1, the first design. The first design looked cool and worked in theory, but after I ran the design and my force calculations by another engineer, he helped me realize that I could solve the problem more effectively with an entirely different design. So I scrapped that and went back to the drawing board with a fresh perspective. The next design I came up with was sleeker, cheaper, and much more effective at solving the crimp force problem with less input force. That design is image 2. The tricky part for me at first was calculating the crimp force at the jaws based on the output from the air pressure actuator. This is what I got wrong with the first design so I wanted to make sure I taught myself correctly, although I hadnâ€™t taken any engineering classes up to that point. The real challenge was ensuring that the crimps from my machine would pass the quality control inspection procedure--everything had to pass based on government-required standards. After triple-checking my calculations, I spent the one or two thousand dollars that this comparatively cheap project cost and sent the drawings over to our machine shop. The end product came together like a Lego set--it was particularly satisfying. I also 3D-printed a mount for the final result, image 3, and a safety cover to prevent wandering fingers from accidentally getting crimped. Overall, this product was a great success. Not only were the operators thrilled by the trouble I saved them, but our throughput on these cables improved tremendously.
